About me

Who am I?

Wow... that sounds more like the beginning of an existential crisis, doesn’t it?
Although I still have to figure out my role in the universe and my divine mission, I definitely have an idea about who I am in my daily life.

I’m Susanna, I’m Italian and I don’t like tomatoes. I like to think of myself as a curious and creative person. I like to constantly learn, and if I’m passionate about something, I’ll go ballistic about it.

I have different passions. First and foremost, music: I like to make beats on Ableton, I scratch (yes, that weird sound that you might or might not have heard in some hip hop tracks), I used to DJ, I collect vinyl and I’m always scavenging to find the perfect sample.

Sports: Yelling “mamma mia!’ doesn’t always take the stress away, sometimes I need a better outlet, and that’s why most of the time you can find me boxing or just simply working out at the gym.

Stand-up comedy: I literally listen to Bill Burr’s podcast every day. But he’s not the only one occupying a special place in my heart. There’s also Dave Chappelle, Patrice O’ Neal, Deon Cole, Louis C.K. and many more. The darker the humor, the better.
